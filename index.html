About the Devs

Hi, We are Group 12 from Hull University we compise of James, Jae, Alex, James and Kurt. We are year 1 students studying Computer Science. 4 members of the group stayed on campus and found that navigating the site at first was difficult and still to this date get lost if we need to go to new rooms. we for our group project on how to make a new students life easier and to encorparte our choice of degree we came up with "HUG" which means Hull University Guide. The Idea is to be able to navigate the campus easier than looking at a 2D map.
